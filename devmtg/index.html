Embulk

Developers' Meeting

About

The Embulk developers' meeting is a gathering of Embulk core developers and plugin developers to share the roadmap and changes upcoming in the Embulk core, to share demands to the Embulk core from plugins' viewpoints, and to make consensus on the future plans.

The meeting has been held invitation-based irregularly when important topics occured, so far as of 2019.
